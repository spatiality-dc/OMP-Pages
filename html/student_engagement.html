The Oxfam-Monash Partnership believes in building meaningful connections between students, Oxfam and the development sector more broadly. Firstly, because we believe that by doing so, we help to build a generation of aware, informed and inspired development activists, ready to drive positive global change into the future. And secondly, because we know that by mobilising their energy, creativity and commitment, young people can help to drive positive global change today.
